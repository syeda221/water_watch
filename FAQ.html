Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about reporting water issues.

What is the purpose of this website?

This platform allows users to report, track, and address water-related issues such as leaks, contamination, or supply shortages in their communities.

How can I submit a new water issue report?

Click on the "Report Issue" button, fill out the form with details like location, type of issue, and upload a photo if possible. Then submit it for review.

Who will see my report?

Your report will be visible to local authorities and utility providers responsible for managing water resources in your area.

Is my personal information kept private?

Yes, we prioritize user privacy. Your personal data is only used to contact you regarding your report and will not be shared publicly.
